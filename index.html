Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Welcome to Yangocean (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following data to improve your experience and for analytics purposes:

  • Data you provide during emotional check-ins (mood ratings, EFT rounds, optional notes).
  • Anonymous demographic or activity data (age, education level, sleep quality) linked only via a program-generated user ID.
  • Currently, Yangocean does not collect personal information such as app version, device type, or other device identifiers. In the future, we may collect such information to improve app performance and user experience. Any collection of additional information will be clearly disclosed in an updated privacy policy.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, or phone number.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide insights and help you track emotional trends anonymously.
  • To improve app performance and features.
  • To generate aggregated statistics for internal analytics, health, wellness, or educational research.

All analytics data is anonymized and cannot identify any individual user.

3. Data Security

We use secure cloud services and encryption where applicable to protect your data. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

4. Your Choices

  • You may delete your anonymous user data through the app at any time.
  • You may choose not to provide optional information within the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App can be used by all users, including those under 16. We do not collect or store personally identifiable information from users under 16. Only anonymous activity data (e.g., mood, sleep quality) may be stored for aggregated insights.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses trusted third-party services such as Supabase for data storage. These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle information. See Supabase Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected with a revised “Last updated” date above.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: happycommunity.apps@gmail.com

隐私政策

最后更新：2025年12月

欢迎使用 Yangocean（以下简称“本应用”）。本隐私政策说明您在使用本移动应用时，我们如何收集、使用及保护您的信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

为提升用户体验及进行数据分析，我们可能会收集以下信息：

  • 您在情绪记录过程中提供的数据（情绪评分、EFT 轮次、可选备注）。
  • 匿名的人口或活动数据（如年龄、教育程度、睡眠质量），仅通过系统生成的用户 ID 进行关联。
  • 目前，Yangocean 不收集应用版本、设备类型或其他设备识别信息。未来如为提升应用性能与用户体验而收集相关信息，我们将通过更新隐私政策进行明确说明。

我们不会收集任何可识别个人身份的信息，例如姓名、电子邮件地址或电话号码。

2. 信息的使用方式

  • 以匿名方式提供洞察，帮助用户追踪情绪变化趋势。
  • 用于改进应用性能和功能。
  • 用于生成汇总统计数据，支持内部分析、健康、身心关怀或教育研究。

所有分析数据均为匿名数据，无法识别任何单一用户。

3. 数据安全

我们使用安全的云端服务，并在适当情况下采用加密技术来保护您的数据。但请注意，任何数据存储或传输方式都无法保证绝对安全。

4. 您的选择

  • 您可随时通过应用删除与您匿名用户 ID 相关的数据。
  • 您可选择不提供应用内的可选信息。

5. 儿童隐私

本应用适用于所有用户，包括 16 岁以下的用户。我们不会收集或存储 16 岁以下用户的任何可识别个人身份信息，仅可能存储匿名活动数据（如情绪、睡眠质量）用于汇总分析。

6. 第三方服务

本应用使用可信赖的第三方服务（如 Supabase）进行数据存储。这些服务将根据其各自的隐私政策处理相关信息。请参阅 Supabase 隐私政策。

7. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有变更，将在本页面更新“最后更新”日期。
